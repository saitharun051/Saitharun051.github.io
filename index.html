I'm Sai Thraun Reddy Mulka, a doctoral student with a focus on cybersecurity in the Department of Computer Science at The University of Texas at Dallas. Under the guidance of my academic advisor Chung Hwan Kim. My research primarily explores the critical area of systems and software. My current work centers on the security challenges associated with Peripheral devices, an often underexplored yet vital aspect of cybersecurity. The project is part of the PeriShied.

I completed my Bachelor's of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering in 2023 at Vellore Institute of Technology, Andhra Pradesh, India. I joined The University of Texas at Dallas in August 2023 to pursue my doctoral studies.
